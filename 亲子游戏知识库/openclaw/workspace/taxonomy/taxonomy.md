# Taxonomy

Use the following controlled vocabulary whenever possible.
If a new tag is needed, add it here first before using it widely.

## Age Stages

- `age_00_12m`: 0-12 months
- `age_12_24m`: 12-24 months
- `age_24_36m`: 24-36 months
- `age_3_4y`: 3-4 years
- `age_4_6y`: 4-6 years
- `age_6_plus`: 6+ years

## Scenes

- `indoor`: at home or other indoor setting
- `outdoor`: park, yard, public outdoor space
- `bedtime`: before sleep, calm state preferred
- `rainy_day`: cannot go outside
- `travel`: car, train, airplane
- `waiting`: restaurant, clinic, queue
- `transition`: before meals, before bath, before leaving home
- `group`: siblings or multiple children
- `one_on_one`: one adult and one child

## Skills

- `language`: vocabulary, expression, instruction following
- `attention`: focus, sustained attention, listening
- `sensory`: sound, touch, sight, movement input
- `gross_motor`: large body movement
- `fine_motor`: hand movement and control
- `emotion`: emotional naming, regulation, connection
- `social`: turn-taking, cooperation, empathy
- `creativity`: imagination, pretend play, divergent thinking
- `math_logic`: counting, patterns, sorting, simple reasoning
- `self_care`: routines, independence, everyday participation

## Materials

- `no_material`: no special material required
- `household_items`: cups, spoons, towels, boxes, pillows
- `paper`: paper, cards, sticky notes
- `drawing_tools`: pens, crayons, paint
- `blocks`: blocks, stacking toys
- `books`: picture books or reading material
- `music`: songs, rhythm tools, speakers
- `ball`: balls or rolling toys
- `nature_items`: leaves, stones, sand, water

## Energy Levels

- `low`: calm, quiet, low movement
- `medium`: moderate movement or engagement
- `high`: active body movement, high excitement

## Difficulty

- `easy`: simple instructions, fast start
- `medium`: needs more setup or multi-step guidance
- `hard`: requires stronger attention, coordination, or adult scaffolding

## Adult Effort

- `low`: minimal setup and guidance
- `medium`: some setup or active guidance needed
- `high`: continuous facilitation or larger preparation needed

## Safety Levels

- `low`: general low-risk play
- `medium`: adult close observation needed
- `high`: age or material risk requires explicit caution

## Recommended Expansion Rule

Only add a new controlled value if:

1. it cannot be represented by existing values
2. it appears in at least 5 entries
3. it will help filtering or retrieval
